A New Story

This project is a new franchise. In this world, Mobile Suits exist with Extraordinary abilities. They are known as Gundams. The Gundams and the Normal Moblie Suits live in a time of Unrest, with the Gundam's existence kept secret, as to not shake the statues quo and prevent war.. However this will soon change.

A New Mysterious Gundam, only known by is serial Number,00, has risen with a the mystious power of GN Particles, giving the MS strange ablites. Adding to that the apperance of the Golden Phonix, a mysterious machine of legend that propheches the end of times. How are this gundam and the Phonix connected?
