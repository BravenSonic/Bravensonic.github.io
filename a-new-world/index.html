A New World

The World inhabited by the Mobile suit is large as it is vast. The planet that is the setting for most of the world is known as Veda. It resembles the Earth of the U.C timeline.

Along with Earth, they are space colonies abandoned by the Precursors. Many mobile suits live in these colonies. The Colony Axis is the offical refuge camp for Gundams and where the story begins.
